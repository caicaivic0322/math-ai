# 七年级下册 第二章实数 学习视频视觉规范

## Style Prompt

做一支平和、温暖、像课堂板书一样的初中数学章节微课。画面基于干净练习本纸张、平方根卡片、立方根小方块、实数分类表、数轴、估算区间和比较大小板书。每个场景只讲一个核心点，强调“想逆运算、分清根号、估位置、再比较”的学习主线，让学生看完能区分平方根与算术平方根，理解立方根和无理数，并能把根号数放到数轴上估算大小。

## Colors

- Paper background: `#f7f3e8`
- Ink foreground: `#1f2a2c`
- Teal primary: `#0d8b7d`
- Lemon accent: `#f5c84b`
- Coral warning: `#ef6a5b`
- Soft mint: `#d8efe5`

## Typography

- Headline: `"Noto Sans SC", "PingFang SC", "Microsoft YaHei", sans-serif`
- Body: `"Noto Sans SC", "PingFang SC", "Microsoft YaHei", sans-serif`
- Roots, exponents, intervals, and comparison formulas should be large, dark, and easy to read in motion.

## Motion

- Use calm classroom pacing, not hype pacing.
- Reveal root symbols, number-line points, and comparison formulas sequentially, as if the teacher is writing and标数轴。
- Transitions should feel like turning a notebook page or sliding a soft marker sheet across the board.
- Keep captions, formulas, and number-line positions on screen long enough for students to follow.

## What NOT to Do

- 不要把实数分类做成密集文字墙。
- 不要只给根号计算答案，必须展示“谁平方或立方后得到它”的逆运算依据。
- 不要让根号、指数、数轴标注和字幕互相遮挡。
- 不要使用强烈闪烁、故障风或暗色科技风。
