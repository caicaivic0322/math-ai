# 七年级下册 第一章相交线与平行线 学习视频视觉规范

## Style Prompt

做一支平和、温暖、像课堂板书一样的初中数学章节微课。画面基于干净练习本纸张、相交线十字图、平行线截线图、角度弧线、位置关系卡片和计算板书。每个场景只讲一个核心点，强调“找截线、定位置、用关系”的学习主线，让学生看完能判断邻补角、对顶角、同位角、内错角和同旁内角，并完成简单角度计算与平行判定。

## Colors

- Paper background: `#f7f3e8`
- Ink foreground: `#1f2a2c`
- Teal primary: `#0d8b7d`
- Lemon accent: `#f5c84b`
- Coral warning: `#ef6a5b`
- Soft mint: `#d8efe5`

## Typography

- Headline: `"Noto Sans SC", "PingFang SC", "Microsoft YaHei", sans-serif`
- Body: `"Noto Sans SC", "PingFang SC", "Microsoft YaHei", sans-serif`
- Angle labels, relation formulas, and final answers should stay large, dark, and easy to read in motion.

## Motion

- Use calm classroom pacing, not hype pacing.
- Reveal lines, angle labels, and formulas sequentially, as if the teacher is drawing and圈出关系。
- Transitions should feel like turning a notebook page or sliding a soft marker sheet across the board.
- Keep captions, diagrams, and formulas on screen long enough for students to follow.

## What NOT to Do

- 不要把五种角关系堆成密集定义墙。
- 不要只展示结论，必须展示判断依据：公共边、面对面、截线、内外位置。
- 不要让角度弧线、点名、公式和字幕互相遮挡。
- 不要使用强烈闪烁、故障风或暗色科技风。
