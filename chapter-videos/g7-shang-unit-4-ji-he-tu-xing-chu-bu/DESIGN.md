# 七年级上册 第四章几何图形初步 学习视频视觉规范

## Style Prompt

做一支平和、温暖、像课堂板书一样的初中数学章节微课。画面基于干净练习本纸张、几何图形卡片、直线射线线段草图、角度弧线和平角计算板书。每个场景只讲一个核心点，强调“看图形、抓端点、认角度关系、再计算”的学习主线，让学生看完能区分基本几何图形并完成简单角度题。

## Colors

- Paper background: `#f7f3e8`
- Ink foreground: `#1f2a2c`
- Teal primary: `#0d8b7d`
- Lemon accent: `#f5c84b`
- Coral warning: `#ef6a5b`
- Soft mint: `#d8efe5`

## Typography

- Headline: `"Noto Sans SC", "PingFang SC", "Microsoft YaHei", sans-serif`
- Body: `"Noto Sans SC", "PingFang SC", "Microsoft YaHei", sans-serif`
- Geometry labels and angle formulas should be large, dark, and easy to read in motion.

## Motion

- Use calm classroom pacing, not hype pacing.
- Reveal diagrams and labels sequentially, as if the teacher is drawing on the board.
- Transitions should feel like turning a notebook page or sliding a soft marker sheet across the board.
- Keep captions, diagrams, and formulas on screen long enough for students to follow.

## What NOT to Do

- 不要把几何概念堆成密集定义墙。
- 不要只展示图形名称，必须展示判断依据：空间、平面、端点、角度关系。
- 不要让角度弧线、点名和公式互相遮挡。
- 不要使用强烈闪烁、故障风或暗色科技风。
