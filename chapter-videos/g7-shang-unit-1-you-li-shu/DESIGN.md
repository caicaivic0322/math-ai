# 七年级上册 第一章有理数 学习视频视觉规范

## Style Prompt

做一支初中数学章节学习微课，不像宣传片那样只喊口号，而像清晰、干净、节奏稳定的课堂板书视频。画面基于练习本纸张、数轴、温度计、计算卡片和错题提醒。每个场景只讲一个核心点，标题大，公式清楚，字幕同步出现，让学生边看边跟上思路。

## Colors

- Paper background: `#f7f3e8`
- Ink foreground: `#1f2a2c`
- Teal primary: `#0d8b7d`
- Lemon accent: `#f5c84b`
- Coral warning: `#ef6a5b`
- Soft mint: `#d8efe5`

## Typography

- Headline: `"Noto Sans SC", "PingFang SC", "Microsoft YaHei", sans-serif`
- Body: `"Noto Sans SC", "PingFang SC", "Microsoft YaHei", sans-serif`
- Formula text should be large, dark, and high contrast.

## Motion

- Use calm classroom pacing.
- Scene entrances may be snappy, but formula and step reveals should be sequential.
- Keep subtitles visible long enough to read.
- No glitch, no dark tech effects.

## What NOT to Do

- Do not cram all quiz questions into one video.
- Do not use tiny dashboard text.
- Do not turn formulas into decorative background only.
- Do not replace explanation with generic inspirational copy.
