# 七年级下册 第五章一元一次不等式与一元一次不等式组 学习视频视觉规范

## Style Prompt

做一支平和、温暖、像课堂板书一样的初中数学章节微课。画面基于干净练习本纸张、数轴、空心点与实心点、不等号方向、移项板书和双数轴公共部分示意。每个场景只讲一个核心点，强调“不等式看范围、数轴看端点、负数运算要变号、不等式组取公共部分”的学习主线，让学生看完能理解解集、会解一元一次不等式，并能在数轴上表示不等式组的解集。

## Colors

- Paper background: `#f7f3e8`
- Ink foreground: `#1f2a2c`
- Teal primary: `#0d8b7d`
- Lemon accent: `#f5c84b`
- Coral warning: `#ef6a5b`
- Soft mint: `#d8efe5`

## Typography

- Headline: `"Noto Sans SC", "PingFang SC", "Microsoft YaHei", sans-serif`
- Body: `"Noto Sans SC", "PingFang SC", "Microsoft YaHei", sans-serif`
- 不等式、解集、端点标签和最终答案必须足够大，数字使用等宽数字特性，确保运动中仍能读清。

## Motion

- Use calm classroom pacing, not hype pacing.
- Reveal number lines, endpoint dots, arrows, and formulas sequentially, as if the teacher is drawing and圈出范围。
- Transitions should feel like turning a notebook page or sliding a soft marker sheet across the board.
- Keep captions, number lines, and solution steps on screen long enough for students to follow.
- Keep number-line arrows visually modest: slim range strokes, small arrowheads, and endpoint dots as the main emphasis.

## Teaching Voice

- Use one calm, warm, patient classroom state throughout the lesson.
- Explain the reason behind each rule, not only the rule itself.
- Prefer scene-level oMLX synthesis so the teacher voice does not shift emotion between short sentences.

## What NOT to Do

- 不要把不等式规则堆成密集定义墙。
- 不要只展示答案，必须展示判断依据：端点是否取到、方向往哪边、是否除以负数、是否取公共部分。
- 不要让数轴、公式和字幕互相遮挡。
- 不要使用强烈闪烁、故障风或暗色科技风。
