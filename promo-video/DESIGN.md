# 初中数学 AI 教学推广视频视觉规范

## Style Prompt

做一支清爽明亮、有青春海报感的初中数学学习产品推广片。画面像干净的练习本、课堂板书和轻快的学习卡片结合在一起：纸白背景、细网格、手写式数学符号、清晰的大标题、轻盈但有冲劲的动效。整体表达“数学不再沉重，学习路径清楚，反馈及时”。

## Colors

- Paper background: `#f7f3e8`
- Ink foreground: `#1f2a2c`
- Teal primary: `#0d8b7d`
- Lemon accent: `#f5c84b`
- Coral accent: `#ef6a5b`
- Soft mint: `#d8efe5`

## Typography

- Headline: `"Noto Sans SC", "PingFang SC", "Microsoft YaHei", sans-serif`
- Body: `"Noto Sans SC", "PingFang SC", "Microsoft YaHei", sans-serif`
- Use heavy headline weights and relaxed body leading. No negative letter spacing.

## Motion

- Snappy entrances, 0.35s to 0.75s, with `power3.out`.
- Math marks drift slowly and stay subtle.
- Scene transitions are clean slides and fades, not glitch effects.
- Use finite repeats only.

## What NOT to Do

- No dark blue or purple tech gradients.
- No generic AI chatbot imagery.
- No dense dashboard screenshots.
- No tiny text that cannot be read in a social video feed.
- No beige-only palette; use teal, lemon, coral, and ink contrast.
